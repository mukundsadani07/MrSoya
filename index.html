About Us

Mr.Soya, established in 2019, we provide you the best quality healthy food products in the market so you won't face the lack of protein and other nutritional content in your diet.

Our Motive
Our main aim is to provide an instant protein-rich soy product that is healthy, tasty, and easy to prepare and contains all the essential amino acids and macronutrients that fit today's busy lifestyles.

Our achievements
We have been selected for UnLtd India incubation were 10 ideas from all around India are selected and we were one of them. We were nominated for the Nagpur Mayor innovation awards 2019. We were also offered incubation in NSRCEL and IIM Bangalore.
